Wedding Details
  • The wedding ceremony will be held at the Hapuna Price Hotel, 62-100 Kaunaoa Drive, Kamuela, HI 96743 on the Turtle Point wedding venue. It will start promptly at 5:30 pm.
  • The wedding reception will be held next door in the Garden Room at the Mauna Kea Beach Hotel.
  • There will be a shuttle running from the front door of the Hapuna Price Hotel to the Mauna Kea Beach Hotel. It is approximately a 5 minute shuttle ride from the Hapuna to the Manua Kea.
  • If you do not wish to take the shuttle from the Hapuna Prince Hotel to the Mauna Kea Beach Hotel there is ample parking at both locations. Please note that the parking is valet only.
